DCF Valuation & Scenario Analysis

Problem Statement

The objective of this project is to evaluate whether the company is overvalued or undervalued at its current market price using a DCF model under multiple scenarios.

Data & Assumptions

  • Financial statements from annual reports
  • Revenue growth and margin assumptions
  • WACC and terminal growth assumptions

Model

Download Excel Model

Key Insights

  • Valuation highly sensitive to revenue growth
  • Downside scenario shows limited margin of safety

Conclusion

Avoid at current valuation.
